QMARS — Quality Management And Review System

A national case management system administered by CMS Center for Clinical Standards and Quality (CCSQ), enabling Beneficiary and Family-Centered Care (BFCC) Quality Improvement Networks (QIOs) to track medical records and perform online case review across the U.S.

The system handles Beneficiary Complaints, Hospital Issued Notices of Non-coverage (FFS), Medicare Appeals (MA), Benefits Improvement and Protection Act (BIPA), and Emergency Medical Treatment and Labor Act (EMTALA) cases — ensuring efficient, secure, and well-documented healthcare quality oversight.
